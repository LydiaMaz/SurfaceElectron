# Dipole Potential Studies

Studies of dipole potentials replacing point charge in neon surface electron calculations.

## Configuration
- Electric dipole with moment p = e × 4 Å at depth d = 10 nm
- Electron above neon surface with image charge interactions

## Results

### Single Dipole (ne.py)
```
State 0: E = -18.1 meV (m=0, n=0)
State 1: E = -15.7 meV (m=1, n=0) 
State 2: E = -14.4 meV (m=0, n=1)
State 3: E = -13.5 meV (m=1, n=1)
State 4: E = -13.2 meV (m=0, n=2)
State 5: E = -13.0 meV (m=1, n=2)

E01 separation: 2377.4 μeV
```
